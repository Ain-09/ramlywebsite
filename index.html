RAMLY's OVERVIEW

Ramly Food Processing Sdn Bhd, formerly known as Perusahaan Burger Ramly Mokni Sdn Bhd, was founded in 1984 by the husband and wife team of Dato' Dr. Haji Ramly Mokni and Datin Hajjah Shala Siah Abdul Manap on the noble platform to produce halal, hygienic and quality products.

WHY CHOOSE US?

AUTHENTIC MALAYSIAN TASTE

Distinct flavor profile that many people associate with the country

AFFORDABILITY

Generally priced competitively, making it accessible to a wide range of consumers

HALAL CERTIFIED

A Muslim-owned company, all Ramly products are halal

VARIETY OPTIONS

Beside burgers, Ramly offers a range of processed meat products such as sausages and nuggets
